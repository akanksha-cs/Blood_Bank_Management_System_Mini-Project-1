Top News in 2020

Title description, Aug 12, 2020

Blood banks across India are running dry as the Covid lockdown has hindered the ‘donation drives’ conducted by these institutions regularly. There has been a near-80% drop in collection over the past two weeks, say experts at leading blood banks.

On normal days, leading blood banks such as the Indian Red Cross Society, Rotary Blood Bank and Lions Blood Bank collect 150 to 250 units of blood from voluntary donors at each of the collection /storage facilities spread across the country. Post the lockdown, these figures have dropped to 30 – 40 units per facility. The situation has put hemophilia and thalassemia patients at grave risk.
